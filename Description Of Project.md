## ðŸ§  Technical Features & Functional Components

### **1. Customer-Level Sales Analysis**
Ranked horizontal bar chart visualizing customer sales volume.

Conditional color gradients highlight performance variance.

Integrated with slicers for dynamic filtering by product category, salesperson, and status.

### **2. Region-Wise Revenue Breakdown**

Comparative visualization for East, West, North, and South regions.

Dual-bar representation supports multi-metric comparison (e.g., revenue vs. cost).

Facilitates regional performance modeling and geographic insights.

### **3. Payment Method Segmentation**

Button-based interactive filters for Cash, Credit Card, Debit Card, and E-Wallet.

Enables analysis of customer transaction behavior and payment channel trends.

Supports evaluation of digital vs. manual payment adoption.

### **4. Dynamic Multi-Level Filters**

Slicers included for:

Product Category & Product Name  

Sales Person  

Status  

Region  

These filters trigger real-time visual updates and recalculations across all charts.
